## What this document is

This document publishes the exact wording MNNR LLC uses on its public surfaces (marketing site, buyer briefs, pilot materials, and API documentation) when the subject matter touches law, regulation, cryptography, certifications, government readiness, hosting, insurance, or commercial-readiness posture.

Each entry pairs:

- the **Public Wording** MNNR uses when describing that topic externally, and
- the **Required Caveat** that must accompany the wording wherever it appears.

Prospective customers, partners, regulators, and investor diligence teams may reference this document to verify that the language MNNR uses in market materials matches the language published here. Deviations should be reported to `siliconhillspr@gmail.com`.

## What this document is not

- It is not legal advice.
- It is not a certification, attestation, or third-party audit report.
- It is not a warranty of any regulatory outcome.
- It does not create any contractual obligation. Contractual terms live in signed MSAs, SOWs, DPAs, and pilot agreements.

## Governing principles

1. MNNR publishes claims only when paired with the caveat below.
2. MNNR does not use the words *compliant*, *certified*, *audit-proof*, *bank-grade*, *military-grade*, *guaranteed*, *fully secure*, or *no-risk* on its public surfaces unless the underlying claim is supported by an independent third-party attestation and MNNR can cite that attestation on request.
3. Where MNNR references a third-party name (payment rail, cloud provider, standards body, regulator), the reference is descriptive of market context or interoperability posture only and does not imply partnership, sponsorship, or endorsement unless expressly stated in a signed agreement.
4. MNNR distinguishes between *design*, *in progress*, and *live* — public copy uses the correct tier.
